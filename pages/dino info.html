Information About Dinosaurs

Did you know that there are roughly 700 known species of extinct dinosaurs? Here you can learn about some of them.

    Ankylosaurus Magniventris

    click here to watch a vedio about Ankylosaurus
  • Size: 25 to 35 feet long(from 7 meters to 7.62m) .

  • Diet : Herbivore.

  • Ankylosaurs' tail clubs would not have been much defense against a Tyrannosaurus rex. But their extremely tough armor might have foiled the 8-inch-long (20-centimeter-long) teeth of the king of the dinosaurs.

  • Found in : Ankylosaurus roamed across the North American West

  • Teeth shape :Ankylosaurs had little leaf-shaped teeth for nipping plants, It had few teeth compared to the duckbills and horned dinosaurs.

  • even though they were slow moving, they were hard to attack,becuase Ankylosaurus had spikes on its head and side, a back covered with armored plates, and a tail that ended in a massive tail made of seven bones fused together into a hard and heavy mass so it was it was more spectacularly protected from predators

    Allosaurus

    click here to watch a vedio about Allosaurus
  • Type of dinosaur: large theropod
  • Length: 12 meters
  • Weight: 2000kg
  • Diet: carnivorous Food:Stegosaurus, Diplodocus and others
  • Teeth: The teeth of Allosaurus were 5-10cm long and curved backwards to prevent prey from escaping.
  • Allosaurus had claws measuring up to 8 inches (20 centimeters) long
  • Allosaurus did not have a very large skull or teeth for an animal of its size, but the bones of its skull were flexible. The lower jawbones could bend outward, making a large space in its mouth for holding meat.
  • Found in: Portugal, USA
